Lighting Effects

Transform Your Venue,
Wall to Wall.

Custom uplighting, intelligent moving heads, dance-floor lighting, and our signature low-lying fog effects — built around your night.

▲

Uplighting

Wireless, fully programmable LED uplights wash your venue in any color. Match your palette, fade through the night, or sync with the music.

✶

Monograms

Your initials, custom logo, or wedding hashtag projected on the dance floor or back wall in crisp gobo light.

●

Sparks

Indoor-safe cold spark fountains for grand entrances, first dances, or last-song send-offs. No heat, no smoke, full drama.

☁

Clouds

Our signature low-lying fog effect for first dances. The fog stays on the floor — no smoke alarms, no fog clouds covering the room.

How It Works

Built for your venue.

Every venue is different. We do a walk-through before your event to map sight lines, power, and rigging — so on the night, the lighting feels designed, not draped.

All packages can be added à la carte to any DJ booking, or built into a Steel City or Burgh package.
