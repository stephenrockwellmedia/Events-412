Lighting Effects

Light is what turns a good party into a story your guests retell for years.

UPLIGHTING

Custom uplighting.

Wireless LED uplighting in any color in the spectrum — set to match your décor, change scenes throughout the night, or pulse with the music during the reception.

DANCE FLOOR

Intelligent dance-floor lighting.

Moving heads, color washes, and effects that respond to the room. We program every scene around the energy of the moment — not just a generic light show.

CLOUD

Dancing on the cloud.

Our signature low-lying fog effect transforms your first dance into a cinematic moment. Safe, dry-ice based, and stunning on camera.

MONOGRAM

Custom monogram & pin spots.

Project your custom monogram on the dance floor or wall, and pin-spot your sweetheart table and cake so they pop in every photo.
