Mirrored Visions

The booth that talks back.

A full-length, interactive mirror that walks guests through the shoot with on-screen prompts and animations. They sign their print on the glass, then it slides out the side as a custom-branded keepsake.

It's the centerpiece of the room — guests line up just to see it work.

Open-Air Booth

Big group? No problem.

Our open-air setup fits the whole bridal party. Custom backdrop, professional lighting, and your choice of strip prints or 4×6s — printed in seconds and shared instantly to phones.
